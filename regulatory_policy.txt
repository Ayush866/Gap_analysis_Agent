GLOBAL DATA SECURITY STANDARD (GDSS) v4.0
Authority: Global Digital Compliance Body
Date: 2024-01-01

SECTION 3: ACCESS CONTROL
3.1. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
(a) All employees with access to sensitive customer data MUST use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
(b) SMS-based MFA is strictly prohibited due to security vulnerabilities; only app-based authenticators or hardware keys are permitted.

3.2. Password Complexity
(a) Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
(b) Passwords must be rotated every 90 days.

SECTION 4: DATA RETENTION & DELETION
4.1. Data Minimization
(a) Customer personal data shall not be retained for longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data are processed.
(b) A strict maximum retention period of 5 years from the last customer interaction is mandated, after which data must be permanently anonymized or deleted.

SECTION 5: INCIDENT RESPONSE
5.1. Reporting Timeline
(a) In the event of a data breach, the organization must notify the regulatory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.
(b) Affected customers must be notified within 48 hours if high-risk data (financial, health) is involved.

SECTION 6: ENCRYPTION
6.1. Data at Rest
(a) All sensitive data at rest must be encrypted using AES-256 standard or higher.
