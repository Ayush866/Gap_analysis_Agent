TECHSTART INC. - INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Version: 2.1
Last Updated: 2023-06-15

1.0 ACCESS MANAGEMENT
1.1 Authentication
To ensure the security of our systems, all staff members are encouraged to use strong passwords. 
- Passwords should be at least 8 characters long.
- We recommend changing passwords once a year.
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is optional for general staff but required for admins. We support Google Authenticator and SMS OTPs for convenience.

2.0 DATA STORAGE
2.1 Encryption
We take data security seriously. All customer databases are encrypted using standard AES-128 encryption to ensure performance and security balance.

2.2 Data Retention
We keep customer data indefinitely to ensure we can provide the best historical analysis and support. If a customer specifically requests deletion, we will process it within 30 days.

3.0 INCIDENT MANAGEMENT
3.1 Breach Notification
If a security breach occurs, the security team will investigate immediately. Once the investigation is fully complete and the extent of the damage is known (typically within 7-10 business days), we will inform the relevant authorities and affected users.
